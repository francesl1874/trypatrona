Who it's for

Independent GCMs and small practices.

Solo care managers, or practices of 2–20 carrying a caseload of 15+ clients. Not for hospitals, not for SNFs, not for agencies trying to automate caregivers out of a job. We'd especially like to talk with ALCA members and anyone who's stitched together a workflow out of Google Docs, voice memos, and a shared calendar.

About

Built from real conversations.

Patrona is being built by a small team after watching family members manage elder care on nights and weekends, and sitting with a half-dozen GCMs who all described the same problem in the same words.
